# Forking Guide (Quick Checklist)

Use this checklist when you fork or adapt this template.

1. Replace all personal identifiers (name, links, email, socials).
2. Replace branding visuals (banners/logos) with your own.
3. Keep a visible attribution block (see `NOTICE`).
4. Indicate your changes (one short line is enough).
5. Do not reuse VictorAOPS/AlgorithmOps brand marks (see `TRADEMARK.md`).
